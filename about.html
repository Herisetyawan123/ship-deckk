About ShipDecKK

Learn about our journey, values, and commitment to maritime excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 1995 on the pristine shores of Monaco, ShipDecKK began with a singular mission: to provide unparalleled maritime services to discerning clients around the world.

What started as a boutique yacht brokerage has evolved into a comprehensive maritime solutions provider, offering everything from vessel acquisition to maintenance, charter services, and crew management.

Throughout our journey, we have maintained an unwavering commitment to excellence, integrity, and personalized service—values that continue to define ShipDecKK today.
